At a safety meeting in a large factory, tragedy struck when one of the employees accidentally knocked over a candle that set off a spark and started a small fire. The fire quickly spread throughout the room due to several cans of paint thinner that had been left out as part of the safety demonstration. Fortunately, all of the workers were able to escape unharmed and the fire was eventually extinguished. While the cause of the accident is still under investigation, it serves as a sobering reminder that safety procedures must always be taken seriously in a work environment.
